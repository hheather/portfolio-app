I'm a Creative Technologist with a background in software development and data. Following my growing curisoity into technology's role within the arts, I have pivoted towards exploring emerging technology as an artistic medium through my MSc in Creative Computing at UAL.

My work focuses on designing immersive experiences that use technology both as a medium and as a subject. In doing so, I aim to create a playful space for collaboration and conversation around our ever-evolving relationship with technology.
