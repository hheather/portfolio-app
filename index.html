The Camera Loves You

The camera loves you is an interactive installation depicting a CCTV camera that performs a display of affection towards its audience. By positioning viewers as the subject of this automated admiration, the piece interrogates society's growing dependence on artificial systems in ways that are becoming increasingly personalised and intimate. The work combines a Vision-Language model with a Large Language Model to generate personalised, affectionate responses directed at audience members.

It invites reflection on how such systems shape our feelings, influence our behaviour, and potentially erode our sense of agency.

02

Meta Memory

Meta Memory is a speculative VR installation exploring what digital spaces could look like if we as a society continue to hoard digital waste.

By drawing comparions between computer and human memory, the piece integrates publicly-sourced 3D scans as real-world examples of digital waste. On interaction with each object, they attempt to reason with the user for their continued existence, in the form of a generated poem via the Deepseek API. The more the user interacts, the more the world degrades, making visible the hidden costs of energy consumption in real-life digital platforms.

Exhibited at Peckham Digital, 2025.

Tools & Technologies

  • Unreal Engine 5
  • Virtual Reality
  • Deepseek API
  • Meta Quest 3
  • Worldbuilding
  • Polycam
